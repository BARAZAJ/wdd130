JINJA, UGANDA

Jinja is a city in eastern Uganda. It is located on Africa largest lake - lake Victoria. It is located right on the spot where the world's longest river, the Nile, meets Africa's largest lake. It is commonly known as "the adventure capital of East Africa" because of its robust and thrilling tourism activities. It is where the Nile starts its long journey to Egypt. It is a blend of urbanization and natural beauty.
